LoopTube - YouTube Player



LoopTube is a simple YouTube Player which can be used as free music player!
Once you enter the artist name, LoopTube will create a playlist of YouTube videos and loop it immediately.
LoopTube plays videos without default YouTube app and can play in background and sleep mode.

Input your favorite artist name and enjoy to watch YouTube videos :)
